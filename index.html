Work

Find me on LinkedIn!

Check out my journey and how I got to where I'm at today! I have prior experience that showcase my other great skills. I have over 10 years of experience in various areas of supply chain, where I have developed my analytical skills and the ability to effectively communicate insights and findings. My expertise includes:

  • Inventory Analysis: I currently work as an Inventory Analyst at Target, where I collaborate with stakeholders to ensure available product and on-time delivery while determining optimal inventory levels and reorder points. I also manage domestic and import items, create orders for the upcoming season/ads, and utilize replenishment systems to drive inventory positioning strategies. I also engage with BU where we would discuss status and address any concerns, risk items, and/or upcoming topics. I use a variety of metrics to actively work and identify root cause to solve inventory positioning defects (ex. low/heavy stocked items) and maintain a weekly POG OOS% within goal of 6.7%. I also Compare POG OOS metrics in terms of basis points (BPs) and Identify top items OOS per subclass, UPI, Digital Purchasability, Item Level Compliance.
  • Production Planning: In my previous role as a Production Planner (Contract) at Laserstar, I planned and coordinated workflow based on consumer demand, conducted meetings for upcoming production schedules, communicated with buyers on needed inventory parts, provided work orders to appropriate personnel, and ensured material availability.
  • Forecasting and Vendor Management: As an Inventory Planner at Hercules Sealing Products, I planned and coordinated workflow, consolidated shop orders, maintained OOS under 10%, assessed and maintained inventory part configurations, forecasted future demand, and created analysis reports to address customer usage and buyer shortage component parts. I also Communicate with numerous departments and/or weekly meetings to address stock outs and/or customer order issues. I also mass data upload including supplier for purchase parts and material costs updates and monitor and manage inventory levels.
  • Procurement and Purchase Analysis: In my role as a Buyer/Planner (Contract) at Hercules Sealing Products, I assisted with allocating and purchasing product for a new location while maintaining supply levels. I created POs, DOs, and purchase requisitions and maintained communication with the appropriate parties to ensure on time deliveries. I also managed relationships with vendors and negotiated inventory orders. I also created supplier for purchase parts when needed including duplicating existing parts for the new facility. I conducted work within IFS Prod 9 an ERP system and maintained an understanding of safety stock, MOQ, and minimum line quantities when creating purchase order to be sent to vendors/suppliers. I created and processed shop orders to be dropped to the warehouse floor to assist with customer and supply demand and schedule shipments to be delivered in accordance with logistics availability.

I am well-versed in various ERP systems like AS400 and SAP and am proficient in data analysis and reporting. My training and experience have given me the skills to analyze large datasets, create detailed reports and visualizations, and automate and streamline data processing. My passion for data and my willingness to learn make me well-suited to take on any data-related challenge.
